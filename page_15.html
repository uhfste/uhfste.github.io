Respiratory Manifestations in sheep,causes along with potential treatments:

Coughing:

1. Lungworms - Treat with anthelmintics like ivermectin, levamisole, or benzimidazoles.

2. Pasteurellosis - Treat with antibiotics like penicillin or tetracyclines. Vaccines can help prevent infection.

3. Parainfluenza type 3 virus - Supportive care and antibiotics for secondary infections. Vaccines can help prevent infection.

4. Foreign body aspiration - Remove obstructions, antibiotics if needed.

Nasal Discharge:

1. Pasteurellosis

2. Mycoplasmosis - Treat with antibiotics like tetracyclines or macrolides. Vaccines can help prevent infection.

3. Viral infections like contagious ecthyma

Pneumonia:

1. Mannheimia haemolytica - Treat with antibiotics like penicillin, tetracyclines, or fluoroquinolones. Vaccines can help prevent infection.

2. Pasteurella multocida

3. Mycoplasma species

4. Lungworms

5. Foreign body aspiration
